## Custom Weapons

### Camp

- Spawns a new [Gangster] or [Gangster Hulk] every 12 seconds.
- Has 3% chance of spawning a boss ([Kingpin], [Bobby] or [Jo]) intead.
- Gangsters guard and protect the camping area.
- Gangsters go out and attack other teams once the camp has 7 or more members.
- Gangsters with less than 30 HP flee back to the tent to heal.

<img width='500' src='https://media.giphy.com/media/M35GitffVrXI3XRc3h/giphy.gif' />

### Chicken

- Has 1 HP and 2 ATK
- Guards and attacks any enemies nearby
- Has 25% chance of making the enemy fall after pecking them.

<img width='500' src='https://media.giphy.com/media/gtfBVVcWfIE80ReAl6/giphy.gif' />

### Fire Trap

Traps the player with a rope and sets them on fire.

<img width='500' src='https://media.giphy.com/media/yUk8Vycd4iyIs3jSg7/giphy.gif' />

### Lasersweeper

- A normal sweeper that shoots laser beam occasionally if it has a good view.
- Deals continuous damage to any objects in the trajectory.
- Has a chance of setting objects on fire.

<img width='500' src='https://media.giphy.com/media/ITSuhEl7SzaSYRBUR8/giphy.gif' />

### Shotgun Trap

Shoots nearby players in mid air. It is activated if you jump over instead of stepping on it like
the other traps.

<img width='500' src='https://media.giphy.com/media/kIjgBI4LVhVU5c2Z9R/giphy.gif' />
<img width='500' src='https://media.giphy.com/media/iVloisB7AL2UlIdYUF/giphy.gif' />

### Tripwire

Trips the player and drops a grenade next to them.

<img width='500' src='https://media.giphy.com/media/6AaUq4WYHTlJmxf1op/giphy.gif' />

### Turret

- Searches and fires enemies.
- Has 300 rounds.
- Has 3 types of damage:
  - Sensor damage: shoots everyone including allies.
  - Controller damage: rotates randomly.
  - Rotor damage: stops rotating.
  - Barrel damage: stops firing.

<img width='500' src='https://media.giphy.com/media/NDGaFtWLSmzztA1R2r/giphy.gif' />
